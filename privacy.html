Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8 2025

1. Information We Collect

Nikomo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from your device. All app data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data for us to use. Your app usage, preferences, and timer settings are stored locally on your device and remain private to you.

3. Subscription Data

Subscription information is handled by Apple's StoreKit and subject to Apple's privacy policy.

4. Data Security

Since Nikomo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, there is no user data for us to secure. All app data remains on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security measures. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through their secure StoreKit system, and we do not have access to any payment information.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at delmadeworld@gmail.com.
